Product Description

The 400W Hybrid Solar Inverter is an intelligent, compact power solution that combines solar and grid charging to power small appliances, lights, fans, and mobile devices. Ideal for off-grid cabins, backup power, and basic home setups, it integrates a solar charge controller and inverter into one sleek unit. Reliable, affordable, and easy to install — a great entry into solar power.

Product Specifications

  • Rated Power: 400W
  • Input Voltage: 12V DC (solar & battery)
  • Solar Input: Up to 18V/10A (recommended panel: 100W-400W)
  • AC Charging: 100-240V AC (auto-switch with solar)
  • Output Voltage: 220V AC ± 10%
  • Waveform: Modified sine wave
  • Battery Type: 12V Lead-acid or Lithium compatible
  • Protection: Overload, short circuit, low battery, overcharge
  • Display: LED indicators for battery, charging, and output status
  • Cooling: Built-in silent fan
  • Use Cases: Small homes, solar kiosks, emergency backup
  • Installation: Plug-and-play with wall mounting option
