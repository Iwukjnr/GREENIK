Product Description

The 300W Solar Panel Kit is a versatile power generation package for small homes, cabins, RVs, and mobile solar setups. It includes a high-efficiency monocrystalline solar panel, MPPT charge controller, mounting brackets, and connecting cables — everything you need to get started with clean energy. Designed to charge 12V/24V battery systems efficiently, this kit is perfect for lighting, DC fans, routers, and more.

Product Specifications

  • Panel Type: 300W monocrystalline solar panel
  • Peak Power: 300W (±3%)
  • Voltage (Vmp): ~18V (12V systems) / ~36V (24V systems)
  • Max Power Current (Imp): ~8.3A
  • Charge Controller: 30A MPPT (auto 12V/24V detection)
  • Battery Compatibility: 12V/24V lead-acid or LiFePO₄ batteries
  • Wiring: 2x 5m PV cables (MC4 connectors)
  • Mounting: Z-brackets or adjustable roof stands (included)
  • Protection: Overload, reverse polarity, overcharge, short circuit
  • Efficiency: MPPT tracking efficiency ≥ 98%
  • Operating Temp: -20°C to 60°C
  • Ideal Use: Lights, routers, DC appliances, backup systems
  • Expansion: Chainable for 600W-1200W systems
  • Warranty: 10 years panel, 1-2 years controller
