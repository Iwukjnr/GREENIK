Product Description

The Flexible 50W Solar Panel is a lightweight, bendable solution for powering small electronics in tight or curved spaces. Designed for portability and ease of installation, it's perfect for RV roofs, tents, boats, backpacks, or emergency kits. Its weather-resistant laminate and monocrystalline cells provide reliable off-grid power even in tough environments. Ideal for mobile lifestyles and minimalist solar needs.

Product Specifications

  • Power Output: 50W (±5%)
  • Cell Type: Monocrystalline (high-efficiency)
  • Voltage (Vmp): 18V
  • Current (Imp): 2.78A
  • Open Circuit Voltage (Voc): ~21.6V
  • Short Circuit Current (Isc): ~3A
  • Dimensions: ~540mm x 670mm x 2.5mm
  • Weight: ~1.2kg
  • Material: PET/EVA laminate with semi-flexible plastic backing
  • Bend Radius: Up to 30° (non-cracking)
  • Mounting: Eyelets and adhesive back (no frame needed)
  • Applications: 12V battery charging, emergency kits, RVs, boats, backpacks
  • Connector: MC4 or USB (model dependent)
  • Warranty: 1 year limited (manufacturer-dependent)
