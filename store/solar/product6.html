Product Description

The 600W Solar Home System is a complete off-grid power solution for homes, small businesses, cabins, or rural areas. It includes a 600W solar inverter, solar panels, battery storage, and a charge controller — all preconfigured to supply reliable, uninterrupted electricity for lighting, fans, phone charging, TVs, and more. Ideal for areas with unstable or no grid access. All-in-one. Plug-and-play. Solar independence simplified.

Product Specifications

  • System Capacity: 600W (inverter rated)
  • Solar Panels: 2x 150W or 3x 200W monocrystalline panels
  • Inverter Type: 600W hybrid inverter (12V input, 220V output)
  • Battery: 12V 100Ah or 200Ah deep cycle (GEL or LiFePO₄)
  • Charge Controller: 20A-30A PWM/MPPT included
  • AC Output: 220V modified sine wave or pure sine wave
  • Ports: 2-4 AC sockets + 2-4 USB ports for mobile charging
  • Load Support: Lights, fans, TV, decoder, laptop, phone chargers
  • Backup Time: 4-10 hours depending on load
  • Mounting Accessories: Panel stands, cables, connectors included
  • Protection: Overload, overcharge, deep discharge, surge
  • Installation: DIY or technician-assisted (manual included)
  • Warranty: 1-2 years on core components
