Product Description

The Digital Anemometer is a handheld wind speed meter designed for accurately measuring air velocity, temperature, and wind flow. Ideal for outdoor enthusiasts, HVAC technicians, drone pilots, and renewable energy planners, this compact device delivers quick, reliable wind data in real time. A must-have for site assessments and field diagnostics.

Product Specifications

  • Measurement Range: 0.3 - 30 m/s (1 - 108 km/h)
  • Units: m/s, km/h, ft/min, knots, mph
  • Temperature Range: -10°C to 45°C
  • Accuracy: ±5%
  • Display: Backlit LCD with dual readout
  • Functions: Max/Min/Average wind speed, wind chill
  • Memory: Stores last reading
  • Power: 1x 3V CR2032 battery (included)
  • Auto-Off: After 5 minutes of inactivity
  • Weight: ~90g
  • Material: ABS plastic body with rubber grip
  • Included: Carry pouch and lanyard
  • Use Cases: Weather stations, wind farm planning, HVAC airflow, drone flying
  • Warranty: 6-12 months (manufacturer-dependent)
