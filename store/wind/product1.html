Product Description

The 400W Wind Turbine Generator Kit (12V/24V) is a compact, efficient, and low-noise solution for generating off-grid electricity in rural homes, RVs, boats, or cabins. Designed for low to medium wind conditions, it features a durable 3-blade turbine and a smart hybrid-ready controller for battery charging. A great choice for beginners and pros alike seeking reliable renewable energy.

Product Specifications

  • Rated Power: 400W
  • Rated Voltage: 12V / 24V (auto-switching)
  • Start-up Wind Speed: 2.5 m/s
  • Rated Wind Speed: 11-12 m/s
  • Max Wind Speed: 45 m/s
  • Blades: 3 - nylon fiber reinforced
  • Rotor Diameter: ~1.2 meters
  • Generator Type: 3-phase permanent magnet AC generator
  • Controller: MPPT wind charge controller (12/24V)
  • Mounting: Flange-type base (tower not included)

  • Application: Cabins, boats, battery charging, hybrid solar-wind setups
