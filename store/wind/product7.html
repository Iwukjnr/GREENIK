Product Description

The Wind & Solar Hybrid Inverter is a smart off-grid solution that combines wind and solar energy to provide seamless, clean, and reliable electricity. Ranging from 1.5kVA to 5kVA, this inverter supports simultaneous input from both power sources and intelligently manages charging, load output, and battery protection. Ideal for homes, farms, schools, and rural areas needing 24/7 hybrid energy.

Product Specifications

  • Power Ratings: 1.5kVA, 3kVA, 5kVA (model dependent)
  • Input Sources: Solar panels + Wind turbine (DC input)
  • Max Solar Input: 1000W - 3000W
  • Max Wind Input: 400W - 1500W (48V wind turbines)
  • Battery Support: 12V / 24V / 48V (auto-detect based on model)
  • Inverter Type: Pure sine wave
  • Output Voltage: 220V AC ±5%
  • Waveform: Pure sine wave (safe for appliances)
  • Charge Controller: Built-in MPPT (solar) + wind controller
  • Display: LCD (real-time battery, load, and source info)
  • Cooling: Intelligent fan cooling system
  • Protections: Overload, short circuit, overcharge, over-discharge, reverse polarity
  • Use Cases: Off-grid homes, solar-wind hybrid systems, agricultural & telecom sites
  • Warranty: 12-24 months depending on brand
