Product Description

Reusable Beeswax Food Wraps are a sustainable, natural alternative to plastic cling film. Made from cotton fabric infused with beeswax, jojoba oil, and tree resin, they mold easily to cover bowls or wrap snacks and leftovers. They're washable, reusable, and compostable — keeping your food fresh while reducing single-use plastic waste. Perfect for eco-friendly kitchens, lunchboxes, and zero-waste living.

  • Reusable: Yes, up to 6-12 months with proper care
