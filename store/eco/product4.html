Product Description

Bring life to your garden, pond, or birdbath with the Solar-Powered Water Pump (Mini Fountain) - an eco-friendly and maintenance-free solution for decorative water features. Powered entirely by sunlight, it requires no batteries or wiring. Simply place the panel in direct sunlight, and enjoy a steady flow of water that adds beauty and movement to your outdoor space. Perfect for bird lovers, garden enthusiasts, or anyone seeking a peaceful, natural vibe in their home or outdoor area.
