Product Description

The Smart Thermometer Plug for Appliances is a precision smart switch that controls your device based on temperature. Simply plug in your heater, fan, freezer, or incubator, set your desired temperature range, and let the thermostat do the rest. With a digital screen, auto shutoff, and real-time monitoring, it ensures your appliances run only when needed — saving energy and protecting your environment. Perfect for greenhouses, fish tanks, poultry farms, or temperature-sensitive devices.
