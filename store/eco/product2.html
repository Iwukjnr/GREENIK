Product Description

The Smart Power Strip (Energy Monitoring) transforms any standard outlet into a connected, energy-saving hub. Featuring four individually controllable AC sockets, USB charging ports, and real-time power consumption tracking, it lets you see exactly how much each device costs to run—and cut wasted electricity with a tap in the companion app. Voice-compatible with Alexa and Google Assistant, surge-protected, and built with flame-retardant housing, this power strip is the perfect upgrade for eco-conscious homes, offices, or gaming setups.
