Product Description

The Solar Phone Charging Kit (5-in-1 Cable) is your all-in-one power solution for charging devices on the go. Equipped with a high-efficiency solar panel and a multi-head USB cable (Lightning, USB-C, Micro-USB), it powers up phones, earbuds, torches, and other low-voltage devices without relying on the grid. Compact and portable, this kit is perfect for travel, rural areas, students, market stalls, or emergency backup use.
