Product Description

The Portable EV Charger (3.5kW, Plug & Play) is a convenient, travel-ready charging solution for electric vehicle owners. Designed to plug directly into a standard wall socket, this charger offers reliable 3.5kW charging on the go — at home, work, or during travel. Lightweight yet durable, it includes built-in safety protections and a Type 2 connector for universal compatibility. Ideal for personal use, backup charging, and mobile fleets.
