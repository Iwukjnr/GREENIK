Product Description

The EV Charging Cable (Type 2 to Type 2) is a reliable, high-quality cable designed to connect your electric vehicle to public or home charging stations with Type 2 sockets. Compatible with most modern EVs and plug-in hybrids in Europe and other IEC regions, this cable supports fast Mode 3 charging and is built to endure daily use with strong insulation and weather resistance. A must-have accessory for convenient and portable EV charging.
