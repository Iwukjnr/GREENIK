Product Description

The 7kW Home EV Charger (Wall-Mounted) is a sleek, powerful solution for fast and efficient electric vehicle charging at home. Compatible with all standard EVs using Type 2 connectors, this unit safely charges your car overnight or during the day while saving on public charging costs.
