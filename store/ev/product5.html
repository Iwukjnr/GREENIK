Product Description

The Solar-Powered EV Charger is an eco-smart charging system that combines clean, renewable solar energy with advanced electric vehicle charging technology. Designed for off-grid or hybrid power setups, it allows you to charge your EV directly from the sun using rooftop or standalone solar panels. Ideal for homes, resorts, eco-parks, and rural installations where grid electricity may be unstable or unavailable.
